We're the Computer Club of Western Michigan University.

Who

Anyone and everyone is welcome. Our group of members and alumni provide a great resource to learn and network, with positions in the school as well as in jobs across the country.

What

We deal in computers, electronics, and technology. If you have an interest in anything computer related you can find like-minded individuals here which you can teach and learn with.

Why

If you have any interest in computers we offer a great way to meet, talk, learn, and teach other WMU students and alumni. Not everyone is interested in talking about technology, but at the CCoWMU you will always have an attentive audience.

When & Where

Our office is in Kohrman Hall, Room 2244. You can drop by any time during our posted office hours, or during our weekly meeting, every Thursday at 6pm. When you decide to join, you will recieve full membership benefits.

Still not convinced?

Our members give presentations and do write-ups about intersting work they do; you can read about it in our regular blog posts and even see what a meeting might entail by checking out our meeting minutes.
